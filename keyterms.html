Key Terms

Samsara: An individual soul or spirit is born repeatedly until moksha is achieved.

Karma: The sum of a person's actions in this and previous states of existence, viewed as deciding their fate in future existences.

Jainism: A non-theistic religion from 6th century BC India that teaches non-violence and self-improvement for salvation.

Moksha: The freedom from the eternal cycle of life, death, and rebirth.
